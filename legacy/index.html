WOX-Bin

Your snippets, organized.

WOX-Bin is a simple paste and notes app: save code, configs, and text in one place. Use it on this device only, or sign in to sync across devices. No ads, no clutter—just paste and go.

No account? No problem. You can start pasting right away and everything stays on your device.

Why WOX-Bin?

📁

Folders & tags

Organize pastes into folders and add tags. Find anything quickly with search and filters (All, Favorites, Recent, Archive).

🎨

Syntax highlighting

Code in JavaScript, Python, HTML, and 20+ languages with readable colors. Toggle line numbers, word wrap, and themes.

🔒

Private or shareable

Keep pastes private, unlisted (link-only), or public. Add passwords or burn-after-read. Share via link, QR code, or embed.

☁️

Optional account

Sign in to save pastes on the server and access them from any device. Your data, your choice.

📤

Export & backup

Download as .txt, .md, or .html. Export everything as JSON. Import from file or from any URL.

⌨️

Keyboard friendly

New paste (Ctrl+N), Save (Ctrl+S), Find (Ctrl+F). Press ? in the app for all shortcuts.

How it works

  1. Click “Get started” — You’re in the app. No signup required.
  2. Create a paste — Type or paste text, add a title, choose a folder and language. You can also drop a file to create a new paste.
  3. Organize — Use folders, tags, favorites, and archive. Sort by newest, oldest, title, or views.
  4. Share or keep private — Copy link, share with optional read-only link, QR code, or embed. Or keep everything local.

Quick tips

  • Use Save as template to reuse a paste as a starting point; open it from Templates.
  • Use History to restore a previous version of the current paste.
  • Use Import URL to fetch content from a URL and create a new paste in one click.
  • Turn on Line numbers and Word wrap in the editor options for a better reading experience.

Privacy & data

When you use WOX-Bin without an account, all pastes are stored only in your browser (localStorage). When you sign in, pastes are stored on the server you connect to. We don’t track you; your data stays in your browser or on your server.
